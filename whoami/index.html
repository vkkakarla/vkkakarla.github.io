I’m a full-time Penetration Tester dedicated to the art of breaking things to make them stronger. My journey into cybersecurity wasn’t just about getting a job; it was driven by a fundamental curiosity about how systems work and more importantly, how their intended controls fail when pushed beyond their limits.

Professional Credentials#

I believe in continuous validation of skill. My methodology is backed by industry-standard certifications that emphasise hands-on capability:

Offensive Security Certified Professional Verify#

The OSCP is my foundation in offensive methodology. It represents my ability to perform enumeration, exploit development, and privilege escalation under high-pressure, time-sensitive environments.

CREST Practitioner Security Analyst Verify#

A prerequisite for the CRT, this focused on the theoretical underpinnings of security assessment, ensuring a rigorous understanding of the “why” behind the “how.”

CREST Registered Tester Verify#

Equivalent to a high standard of professional competency in the UK/EU market. This certification validates my skills in identifying vulnerabilities in network infrastructure and providing industry-standard reporting.

My Philosophy#

In the world of offensive security, it’s easy to get lost in the “cool” exploits. However, I believe the true value of a penetration tester lies in communication. Finding a vulnerability is only half the job; the other half is articulating the risk to stakeholders and providing a clear, actionable roadmap for remediation.

Beyond the Terminal!#

When I’m not in a shell or writing reports, you can usually find me random hiking spots, cafè’s and in my man cave. I am a frequent flyer on HackTheBox. I use the platform to stay sharp on the latest CVEs and complex pivoting scenarios.
